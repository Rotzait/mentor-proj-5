What is Money, and how will it help me?

Money offers the power to acquire things and improve the material lifestyle of people. The lack of it can become a problem, especially for people living in cities.

Is Money free?

Yes, life offers both free and normal Money challenges, with the free option being assigned at birth if your family's rich. If that's not the case, you'll have to work for it.
